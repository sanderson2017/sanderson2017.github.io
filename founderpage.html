Meet the Founder -

Seth Anderson

Management Consultant Turned Entrepreneur

Brief Background

Seth completed his undergraduate studies at Morehouse College recieving a degree in business adminstration in 2010. At the age of 19, while working two jobs in addition to being a full-time student, he purchased his first real estate property in Atlanta, GA. Shortly after he entered the rental real estate market acquiring several properties in the Atlanta area. Seth is a partner in several investment funds and owns two service based businesses. He is currently pursing a JD/MBA at Northwestern University.
